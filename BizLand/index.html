About

Find Out More About FarmTech

FarmTech is a platform where complex agricultural processes are made simpler and efficient through IT and Software Development

Our History

Our team of six elite Software Developers established FarmTech platform with the aim of automating different agricultural processes so as to optimize agriculture which is the backbone of our economy and livelihoods. Our first ever software the Feed Formulator automates the pearson square thus enabling farmers to formulate home-made animal diets with the available feed ingredients. We aim to Optimize Agriculture by solving more agricultural problems that require our programming knowledge and skills.
